Clear all completed tasks

Click Clear completed to remove all completed tasks.

Edit a task

On your computer, hover over a task and click Edit. Support for editing on mobile coming soon.

Share your list with someone

  1. Click on Share list in the top right.
  2. Copy the link to your clipboard and send it to someone.
  3. When they open the link, your lists will be connected.

If you experience any problems with shared lists, please let me know.

Sync tasks to your phone

  1. Click on Share list in the top right.
  2. Copy the link to your clipboard, send it to yourself, and open it on your phone.
  3. If you’re using Chrome on Android, tap Menu and choose Add to Home screen to save a shortcut to Super Simple Tasks. Learn more about Add to Home screen.

If you experience any problems with syncing, please let me know.

Connect multiple Chrome extensions

As of Super Simple Tasks 3, syncing now uses the built-in sync feature instead of the chrome.storage.sync API. This means you need to manually connect multiple Chrome extensions.

Better support for easily connecting multiple Chrome extensions is coming, however for now you can connect them manually by following these steps:

  1. Click on Share list in the top right.
  2. Copy the the last part of the link that begins with /?share= and send that to yourself.
  3. On your other device, open the extension in a new tab by clicking Open as a tab.
  4. Paste the contents of /?share= at the end of the URL in your address bar.
  5. Hit Enter, and your tasks should now sync.

Example

If your copied share code is this:

/?share=c1428d

Then this is what you should have in the address bar of the second computer:

chrome-extension://ddbbiebdajepgmddlllejnbpfhfpcdpn/?share=c1428d

If you experience any problems with syncing, please let me know.

Disconnect from a shared list

Click Share list in the top right and choose Disconnect from shared list.

Delete a task

On your computer, hover over a task and click Delete. On your phone, swipe a task to delete it.

Print tasks

To print your tasks hit CMD + P (OS X) or CTRL + P (Windows) on your keyboard.
