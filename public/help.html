Clear all completed tasks

Click Clear completed to remove all completed tasks.

Edit a task

On your computer, hover over a task and click Edit. Support for editing on mobile coming soon.

Export tasks

Click Export tasks at the bottom of the page to download your tasks as a JSON object.

Share your list with someone

  1. Click on the Share list icon in the top right.
  2. Copy the link to your clipboard and send it to someone.
  3. When they click the link, their task list will be connected with yours.

If you experience any problems with shared lists, please let me know.

Sync tasks to your phone

  1. Click on the Share list icon in the top right.
  2. Copy the URL to your clipboard and send it to yourself and open it on your phone.
  3. If you’re using Chrome on Android, tap the Menu icon and choose Add to Home Screen to save a shortcut to Super Simple Tasks.

If you experience any problems with syncing, please let me know.

Connect multiple Chrome extensions

As of Super Simple Tasks 3, syncing now uses the built-in sync feature instead of the chrome.storage.sync API. This means you need to manually connect multiple Chrome extensions.

Better support for easily connecting multiple Chrome extensions is coming, however for now you can connect them manually by following these steps:

  1. Click on the Share list icon in the top right.
  2. Copy the the last part of the URL that begins with /?share= and send that to yourself.
  3. On your other device, open the extension in a new tab by clicking the Open in new tab icon.
  4. Paste the /?share= at the end of the URL.
  5. Hit Enter, and your tasks should now sync.

For example, if your copied share code is this:

/?share=67376ee000a58367a06f

Then this is what you should have in the address bar of the second computer:

chrome-extension://ddbbiebdajepgmddlllejnbpfhfpcdpn/?share=67376ee000a58367a06f

Do not simply copy and paste this example as it won’t work.

Disconnect from a shared list

Click on the Share list icon in the top right and click Disconnect from shared list.
